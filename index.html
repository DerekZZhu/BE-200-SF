History and the Built Environment

  • - Historical events and actions are memorialized and remembered by the buildings and monuments that stand in their place.
  • - Intangible heritage such as culture and history can continue living on through tangible heritage, namely the built environment.
  • - These pieces of tangible heritage embody the lives and memories of the collective and of the city. Thus the spirit of a city is represented through the historical tapestry of the built environment.
